Insights:

The above bar graphs show that in general, diamonds with ideal cut and no color have a higher average price.

For diamond cut, there is a big price gap between the ideal ones and the fair ones. Also, signature-ideal ones and very good ones are very close in pricing.

For diamond color, though colorless is the most expensive one, diamonds with a light color are more expensive than diamonds with a little bit of color. The F ones' and G ones' average prices are very close.
